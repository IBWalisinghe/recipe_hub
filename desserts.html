Dessert Recipes

1. Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 2 eggs + 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour

Steps:

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Fold in melted chocolate and flour.
  4. Pour into ramekins and bake for 10–12 min at 200 °C.

2. Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 200g digestive biscuits (crushed)
  • 100g melted butter
  • 400g cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • Strawberry topping

Steps:

  1. Mix crushed biscuits and butter to make the base. Chill.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  3. Fold in whipped cream and pour over the base.
  4. Top with strawberry sauce and chill for 4 hours.
