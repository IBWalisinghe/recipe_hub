1. Garlic Bread Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 French baguette (sliced)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180 Â°C.
  2. Mix butter, garlic, and parsley. Spread on bread slices.
  3. Bake for 8 minutes until golden. Sprinkle cheese if desired.

2. Mini Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp ginger & garlic paste
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Stir-fry vegetables with soy sauce and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Let it cool, then wrap the filling in spring roll wrappers.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown. Serve with dipping sauce.
